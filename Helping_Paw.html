About

The Helping Paw Medical Centre is an NHS Doctor's Surgery located within Squirrel's Rest Refuge for Exhausted Squirrels.
The Medical Centre supports both residents and non-residents, squirrel or otherwise. It is managed by Dr H. Paws (Dr. med),
an experienced General Practitioner, specialising in Gastroenterology, who studied at the Berlin Medical School.

Location

Helping Paw Medical Centre can be found within Squirrel's Rest Refuge for Exhausted Squirrels, in the charming Cheshire village of Norley.
Norley has a pleasant atmosphere and is highly recommended as a location for rest and recuperation, as it boasts an excellent local pub.

Staff

Dr H. Paws is the Senior Partner and sole medical doctor at Helping Paw. He graduated from Berlin Medical School
(Hochschule für Gesundheit und Medizin) in 2016, specialising in gastroenterology, with particular attention to thermo-tactile therapy.

Mr P. Bear is the resident Phlebotomist at the Helping Paw Medical Centre. He has many years of practical experience,
and can draw blood quickly and efficiently with a friendly smile.

Mr B. M. Ouse is the resident Pharmacist at Helping Paw Medical Centre, and keeps tight control over our well-stocked medical supplies.
He is capable of dispensing a variety of medicines, including digestive aids, painkillers, opiates, malaria medication and alcohol.

The rest of the staff at the Helping Paw Medical Centre are shared with Squirrel's Rest.
