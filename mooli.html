Mooli

A mysterious vegetable.

Inspect closely to reveal its secrets.

Mooli is a fourth-hand-tat-warehouse-cum-art-gallery. It has existed, perhaps, in an abandoned shipping container in Falmouth, Cornwall, although it's physical presence there has always been questionable.

Mooli is carefully curated and mismanaged by the wise philosopher known as Mr B. M. X. Ouse.

For tax reasons, Mooli is now located within a small rodent sanctuary deep in the Cheshire countryside.
